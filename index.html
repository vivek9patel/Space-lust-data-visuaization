This visualization shows the exoplanets with distance from Earth.

  • The size of the circle represents the radius of the planet with respect to Earth.
  • The color of the circle represents the type of planet.
  • The angle shows the mass of the planet with respect to Earth. Which means, the more the mass the more the angle.
  • You can see circluar boundry with it's width in increasing order, which shows the increasing mass with respect to Earth.
  • You can hover over exoplanets to see the name & distnace of the planet.
  • Scroll bottom to see more filters!
